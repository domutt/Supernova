SUPERNOVA

The world’s largest network of leading digital animators and artists
Supernova is the first major outdoor festival of digital animation and art, initiated by Denver Digerati, Arts and Venues Denver, and the Denver Theatre District in 2016. The festival utilizes a robust network Denver Digerati has developed over five years through an ongoing series of “Friday Flash programs” that have brought advances in digital animation and motion art to a network of public LED screens situated in Denver’s downtown core as a new public art viewing experience. The program promotes the dynamic, rapidly advancing impact technology is having in art throughout the world today. Denver is the only city in the world to support a sustainable protocol that presents digital animation as public art in a meaningful and ongoing context via outdoor LED Screens.

CORE FESTIVAL ELEMENTS

  • Seven hours of continuous screenings of diverse and dynamic short-form digital content on our massive LED screen located at the corner of 14th and Champa, as well as on a large-scale temporary LED screen located within the Denver Performing Arts Complex in the heart of downtown Denver.
  • 90 minute Competition Screening on both permanent 14th and Champa LED screen as well as temporary LED screen situated within the Galleria at Denver Performing Arts Complex (DPAC). Cash prize awards to be selected by three distinguished guest jurors chosen for their prestige within digital media on an international level.
  • Education forum featuring Juror presentations, panel discussions and dialogue with professionals associated with the festival and program screenings.
  • Preview Launch party hosted by Culturehaus on Friday evening, September 22nd taking place at ATC DEN, the city's premier new event space, located on Upper Larimer Street. Invitation only!
